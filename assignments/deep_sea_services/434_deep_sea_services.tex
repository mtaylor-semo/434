\subsubsection*{Deep sea ecosystem functions and services (20~pts.)}


Download and read through the scientific publication by A.\,R.\,Thurber~et~al.~2014 posted on the course Canvas page. Answer the following questions. Be sure to use your own words. Do not copy or quote directly from the paper or other source. Each question is worth 5 points.

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]


\item Thurber~et~al. discuss “supporting and regulatory” functions and services, and “provisioning” functions and services. Explain what you think is the difference between these two types of functions and services.

\item Describe and summarize one supporting and regulatory function and service. Be sure your summary explains the ecosystem function \emph{and} the service that humans derive from the function. Remember they are two different things.

\item Describe and summarize one provisioning function and service. Be sure your summary explains the ecosystem function \emph{and} the service that humans derive from the function. 

\item For each service, explain how you would try to get the general public to understand the importance of each. Be creative. Be specific.

\end{enumerate}

Upload your answers by the due date given for the drop box.
